Privacy Policy

At Taikun Strategies, we are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with U.S. federal and state data protection laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines. We also voluntarily align with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles to uphold global best practices, even though we are based in the USA.

Information We Collect

We collect personal data such as name, email, and inquiry details through our contact form to respond to your requests. We do not collect sensitive information without consent, in compliance with CCPA and FTC requirements.

How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely for business communications, such as responding to inquiries. We do not share it with third parties without consent, except for service providers under strict agreements, adhering to CCPA opt-out provisions and FTC fair information practices.

Your Rights

Under U.S. laws like the CCPA, you have rights to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt-out of data sales. You may also request access or correction of your data under FTC guidelines. Contact info@taikunstrategies.com to exercise these rights.

Security and Cookies

We use secure hosting and minimal cookies for functionality, complying with FTC security standards. No tracking cookies are used.
